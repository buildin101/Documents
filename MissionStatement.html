Mission Statement

What do we do?

101 Building Services encourages and supports the growth and development of all employees in attaining their goals, allowing the employees to focus entirely on satisfying the needs of the customer. 101 Building Services is 100% employee owned individual success is integral to company success.

How do we do it?

We provide an environment for individual success in five ways:

Family First. 101 Building Services is a family. The company provides lunch for everyone on Friday to celebrate the week past and the coming weekend, children's activities. This is a company meeting in a very informal setting but it is a meeting.

Challenge and competition. 101 Building Services is the best in the industry and is fiercly competitive in the industry, internal competition is a normal part of any jobsite and is encoureged to the extent that it remains friendly. People live best when they are challenged, 101 Building Services encourages and rewards excellence.

101 Building Services is a "Data Driven", "Open Information" company. Everyone employed longer than 1 year has a stake in the company and has a "Right to Know" all information. Collecting information and making that information available is a top priority. As much information as possible is available publicly while protecting the clients privacy. We are not the best because we keep secrets, we are the best because we understand the game we play.

Education and Training. We encourage and support the growth of individuals whether work related or personal. We provide internal training frequently and individual or group community college attendance.
